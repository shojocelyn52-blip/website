Northern & Western Districts

Welcome, Local Tourists! 🌎

Welcome to Taste of Belize — your ultimate guide to the best dining experiences across our beautiful country! Whether you are a foodie, an adventurer, or simply looking for a great meal, Belize has something special for every palate. This page invites you to explore the wonderful restaurants found in the Northern and Western Districts of Belize — Corozal, Orange Walk, and Cayo. Get ready to taste the culture, the history, and the heart of Belize on every plate! 🍽

Corozal District

Corozal is a charming town in the far north of Belize, nestled along the shores of Corozal Bay. Known for its rich Maya and mestizo heritage, Corozal offers a peaceful and welcoming atmosphere. The town is famous for its colorful buildings, fresh seafood, and warm community spirit. A visit to Corozal is truly an unforgettable cultural experience!

🍽 Corozo Blues Restaurant

Corozo Blues is one of Corozal's most beloved dining spots, offering a relaxed waterfront atmosphere and a menu full of authentic Belizean flavors. Whether you're stopping in for lunch or a romantic dinner, Corozo Blues promises a memorable experience with every bite.

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍣 Grilled Red Snapper with Rice and Beans
  • 🍖 Belizean Stew Chicken
  • 🍝 Garnaches and Salbutes
  • 🍷 Fresh Coconut Water
  • 🍻 Sorrel Drink
  • ☕ Local Rum Punch

Orange Walk District

Orange Walk, fondly known as the "Sugar City" of Belize, is located in the heart of northern Belize. It is home to the famous Lamanai Maya ruins and is surrounded by lush sugarcane fields and rich farmland. Orange Walk is a vibrant hub of culture, history, and of course — incredible food!

🍽 Cocina Sabor Restaurant

Cocina Sabor brings the bold and delicious flavors of northern Belize cuisine to your table. This local gem is known for its hearty portions, fresh ingredients, and warm, family-friendly service. From breakfast to dinner, Cocina Sabor is a must-visit when exploring Orange Walk.

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍗 Tamales and Panades
  • 🍖 Relleno Negro (Black Dinner)
  • 🍝 Escabeche (Onion Soup)
  • 🍱 Fry Jacks with Eggs and Cheese
  • 🍷 Horchata
  • ☕ Hot Chocolate

Cayo District

Cayo is the largest district in Belize and a true paradise for nature lovers and adventurers. Surrounded by lush rainforests, ancient Maya ruins like Xunantunich and Caracol, and the stunning Macal River, Cayo is the adventure capital of Belize. After a day of exploring, there is nothing better than a delicious meal at one of Cayo's finest restaurants.

🍽 Guava Limb Restaurant

Guava Limb is a celebrated restaurant in San Ignacio, Cayo, known for its creative fusion of Belizean and international cuisine. Set in a beautiful garden atmosphere, dining at Guava Limb feels like a true escape into nature while enjoying some of the finest food Belize has to offer.

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍖 Cohune Palm Heart Salad
  • 🍣 River Fish with Chimichurri
  • 🍝 Cayo-style Gibnut (Royal Rat)
  • 🍱 Plantain Chips with Guacamole
  • 🍷 Fresh Lime Juice
  • ☕ Guava Punch
