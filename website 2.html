Southern & Eastern Districts

Welcome, Local Tourists! 🌎

Welcome back to Taste of Belize! This page takes you on a delicious journey through the Southern and Eastern Districts of our beautiful country — Belize City, Stann Creek, and Toledo. Each district is unique in its culture, landscape, and cuisine. From the bustling streets of Belize City to the lush rainforests of Toledo, there is a world of flavor waiting for you. Let's explore! 🍽

Belize District

Belize City is the largest city in Belize and the country's commercial and cultural hub. With its vibrant streets, historic swing bridge, and colorful Creole culture, Belize City is a city full of energy and character. It is a melting pot of cultures — Creole, Garifuna, Mestizo, and more — which makes its food scene incredibly diverse and exciting!

🍽 St. George Restaurant

St. George Restaurant is a well-known dining destination in Belize City, offering authentic Belizean cuisine in a warm and welcoming setting. Named after the nearby St. George's Caye — the site of a famous 1798 battle — this restaurant carries the spirit of Belizean pride in every dish it serves.

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍣 Boil Up (Belizean National Dish)
  • 🍖 Creole Stew Fish
  • 🍝 Rice and Beans with Coleslaw
  • 🍱 Johnny Cakes with Eggs
  • 🍷 Seaweed Shake
  • ☕ Belikin Beer

Stann Creek District

Stann Creek, home to the vibrant city of Dangriga, is the cultural heartland of the Garifuna people. Known for its lively Garifuna Settlement Day celebrations, beautiful beaches, and the stunning Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary, Stann Creek is one of Belize's most culturally rich destinations. The food here is deeply rooted in Garifuna tradition and is unlike anything else in Belize!

🍽 Tipsy Tuna Restaurant

Tipsy Tuna is a beloved beachside restaurant and bar in Placencia, Stann Creek, offering stunning views of the Caribbean Sea alongside some seriously amazing food. It's the perfect place to kick back, enjoy the ocean breeze, and treat yourself to fresh seafood and tropical drinks.

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍣 Hudut (Garifuna Fish Stew with Mashed Plantain)
  • 🍖 Grilled Lobster with Garlic Butter
  • 🍝 Cassava Bread with Coconut Fish
  • 🍱 Conch Fritters
  • 🍷 Rum Punch
  • ☕ Coconut Margarita

Toledo District

Toledo is Belize's southernmost district and one of its most untouched and breathtaking regions. Known as the "Land of the Maya", Toledo is home to many Mopan and Kekchi Maya villages, lush jungles, and incredible biodiversity. Toledo is also famous for producing some of the finest cacao in the world, making it a chocolate lover's paradise!

🍽 A Piece of Ground Restaurant

A Piece of Ground is a charming local restaurant in Punta Gorda, Toledo, known for its farm-to-table style cooking using fresh ingredients grown right from the earth. This cozy spot celebrates the rich agricultural roots of Toledo, serving up wholesome and flavorful meals that reflect the true spirit of southern Belize. 🌿

🍴 Signature Dishes & Drinks:

  • 🍖 Slow Cooked Pork with Roasted Vegetables
  • 🍝 Caldo (Maya Chicken Soup)
  • 🍣 Tamales wrapped in Banana Leaves
  • 🍱 Roasted Breadfruit with Butter
  • 🍷 Cacao Tea (Toledo Hot Chocolate)
  • ☕ Fresh Sugarcane Juice
