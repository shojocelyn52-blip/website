🌎 Welcome to Taste of Belize!

Belize is a land of incredible diversity from the lush rainforests of Cayo to the crystal-clear Caribbean waters of Placencia, from the vibrant streets of Belize City to the ancient Maya villages of Toledo. And with that diversity comes a food culture unlike anywhere else in the world.

Taste of Belize is your official guide to the best local restaurants across all 6 districts of our beautiful country, proudly brought to you by the Ministry of Tourism, in collaboration with BTB, BTIA, and Beltraide. Whether you're exploring the north, heading west, or venturing south — there's always an amazing meal waiting for you!
